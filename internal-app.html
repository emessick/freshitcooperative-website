Internal Application

This page describes a private application used only by Fresh IT Cooperative LLC for the operation of its own business. It is not a public service.

Scope

The application referenced by this page is an internal Chief of Staff system used by FITC employees to coordinate operations across our own QuickBooks Online, Microsoft 365, FreshDesk, Restaurant365, Ramp, and related business systems. It accesses only company-owned accounts that have been explicitly authorized by FITC administrators.

Who Can Use It

Access is restricted to authenticated employees and authorized AI agents operating on behalf of Fresh IT Cooperative LLC and Fresh Support LLC. The application is not available to the general public, customers, or third parties.

Data

Data accessed by the application remains within FITC's own tenants and infrastructure. Authentication uses Microsoft Entra ID and OAuth 2.0 against FITC-controlled identities. The application reads accounting and operational data to support internal business workflows; it does not redistribute or sell any data to third parties.

Connecting and Disconnecting

FITC administrators authorize the application's access via standard OAuth 2.0 consent flows in each provider's developer or admin portal. Access can be revoked at any time by removing the application's authorization or rotating the relevant credentials. For QuickBooks Online specifically, authorization can be reviewed and revoked at app.qbo.intuit.com/app/connectedapps.
